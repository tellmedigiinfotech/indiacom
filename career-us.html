Digital Telesales Executive

Indiacom · Dhole Patil Road, Pune (Near Pune Railway Station) · Work Location: In person

Full-time
Join a Company With 37 Years of Trust — and a Fresh New Mission

Indiacom has been a household name in Indian business directories for over three decades, serving 10,000+ businesses across the country. Today, we're on an exciting new journey — helping schools, hospitals, clinics, and local businesses across Pune stand out on Google with cutting-edge visual content.

If you're someone who loves talking to people, enjoys a challenge, and wants to build a real career in digital sales — this role was made for you.

What We Offer Our Clients

We make it easy for businesses to look professional and trustworthy online:

  • Google 360° Virtual Tours — a stunning walkaround of their premises, live on Google Maps
  • Google Business Profile setup and management — so customers can find them easily
  • Reels and AI-driven videos — engaging content they can use across social media and their website

Our clients don't need to know much about technology. We explain everything simply — and that's exactly what you'll be trained to do too.

Your Role at Indiacom

As a Digital Telesales Executive, you'll be the voice of Indiacom. You'll connect with schools, clinics, and small businesses across Pune, introduce them to our services, and guide them from that first phone call to a confirmed booking. Every successful call you make directly helps a local business grow — and puts extra money in your pocket through incentives.

Day-to-day, you will:

  • Call prospects from our ready-made, well-researched Pune database — no cold hunting required
  • Have friendly, informative conversations that help businesses understand how we can help them
  • Fix appointments for our photography and content team to visit the client
  • Follow up consistently and maintain your own pipeline of interested leads
  • Hit monthly targets and celebrate wins with a supportive team around you
Who We're Looking For

You don't need to be a tech expert. You just need to be a great communicator with the drive to succeed.

  • Experience: 0–2 years in telecalling, sales, or customer interaction — freshers are absolutely welcome
  • Languages: Confident in Hindi and English; Marathi is an added advantage
  • You are: Energetic, persuasive, organised, and not afraid of targets
  • We provide complete training on our services, tools, and calling techniques from Day 1
What's in It for You
  • Salary: ₹15,000 – ₹20,000 per month based on your experience
  • Incentives: Attractive performance bonuses on every sale — the more you convert, the more you earn
  • Hands-on training so you're never left figuring things out alone
  • A stable, growing company with a strong brand name behind you
  • Conveniently located office, just minutes from Pune Railway Station
